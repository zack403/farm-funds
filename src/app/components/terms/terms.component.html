Summary of Service

Farmfunds Africa is a tech-enabled Farm agricultural service that sources funds for farmers which serve as one of the product and services created at Farmify Agro Innovations Ltd. Each farm has an amount needed for funding, the possible yields or returns from harvest, and the time duration till harvest with the possibility of getting paid upon a successful harvest; however, if unsuccessful, the subscribers receive their capital back subject to the insurance coverage.

The Subscriber agree that by subscribing to a farm, your contribution is tied and unavailable for the entire duration of the Farming Duration and neither Farmify Agro Innovations Ltd nor the farmers can or will refund your contribution until the successful completion of a Farming Duration.

Any profits from a successful Farming Duration of a Subscriber’s farm are remitted to the electronic wallet on the Platform when the produce is harvested and sold (i.e. upon completion of the Farming Duration).

At the end of a successful Farming Duration, the Subscribers can decide whether to withdraw his/her funds from the electronic wallet on the Platform and transfer to a bank account you provide us with or to subscribe to another farm for a new Duration subject to that Farm’s terms & conditions. At the end of a Farming Duration, the Subscriber’s Expected Returns is disbursed from the profits made on the said farm he/she invested on.
