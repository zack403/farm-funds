Farm Investment Asked Questions

What is Farmfunds Africa?

Farmfunds Africa (Farmify Agro Innovations Ltd: RC 1627509) is an agritech Company focused at empowering African Farmers whilst enabling individual investors earn profits on their investment capital which ultimately helps in strengthening global food security. We pride as Africa’s fastest growing Agritech Company.

Where is your office located?

Our head office is located at 12a, Sanyaolu street. Off Kudirat Abiola way, Oregun. Ikeja, Lagos.

How does it work?

We handle the entire agric value chain "from start to finish" while you sit back and enjoy returns after harvest.

How much is a farm unit?

A unit of Farm varies. It ranges from N40,000 – N1,000,000. You can decide the number of units you want to subscribe to.

How do I make payment?

Payment can be made via the following channels:
a: Debit Card
b: Bank Transfer/Bank Deposit.
c: POS payment at our office.

Where are your farms?

We are currently working with farmers across different regions of the country whilst we are currently setting up one of the largest farm city in West Africa.

How many offices/branches does Farmfunds Africa have?

Presently our head office is in Lagos. However, we are a 100% digital compliant brand. Thus, we are just a click away. You can reach us via our social media platforms

Can I visit your farm?

Yes. Once a quarter, we hold a meeting which is focused on connecting our farmfunders with the farmfunds team to build healthier relationships and more importantly, enable our farmfunders see how their partnership is affecting economic growth and the lives of people.

What is the duration of farm?

All our available farms have their farm cycles which ranges between 100 days to 270 days (9 months). Your selected farm determines your partnership maturity.

Can I withdraw funds before maturity?

No You cannot. However, in extreme cases (death) , your next of kin will be paid your full partnership funds and returns at the end of farm cycle.

At farm maturity, how do I get my funds back?

At maturity, your partnership funds plus its accrued profit is paid back to your local bank account. At this point you can either choose to resubscribe or walk away happily.

How secured is my partnership fund?

All our farms and farmers have a comprehensive insurance cover to mitigate all risks. Your partnership with us is secured. We have a relationship with Leadway Assurance Plc and NAIC (Nigerian Agricultural Insurance Cooperation)

Food Bank Asked Questions

What happens if I don't want my foodstuff for the current month?

Delivery of your food and household items can be extended till the next month(s). For example; we can move your 5% from the month you skipped to the next month, and deliver 10% to cover up for the previous month.

Must I purchase only Farmify Agro products?

Your purchasing power is not limited to Farmify Products alone. You can purchase any foodstuff, groceries, provisions, toiletries etc. from that can be gotten from the market or supermarket for a more reasonable price at your convenience.

Will I be paying for delivery at any point in time?

No, the delivery is free throughout the year.

Which states in Nigeria does the Farmify Market Food Bank service cover?

For now, we cover only Lagos and its environs.

How do I place an order?

Send your list with price and description via your user back end and the email provided to you on our website.

How will I know my delivery date?

Your delivery date will be communicated to you and will be maintained for the rest of the year.

How do I make my partnership payment?

You’re to pay via Bank transfer or Bank deposit. Your proof of payment must be sent to our WhatsApp line: 08097365000 or to the email address: sales@farmfundsafrica.com.

What if there is a change of address for delivery?

You need to notify us a month before delivery.

What if I don’t have anyone at home to receive the package?

The package will be returned and the customer will have to pick it up or will be charged for another delivery.

Will I be charged if I notify Farmify Agro ahead of time that no one will be available to receive the package on the set delivery date?

No, you won’t be charged as long as you notify us on time. You can also move your delivery date to the next day or week without extra charges.

How does the “Food Bank” partnership work?

Sponsor our Greenhouse Vegetable Farm for just N100,000 per unit and get 60% ROI back within the space of 1 year. The 60% will be broken down into 12 parts which means you will be paid 5% of your ROI monthly in form of groceries, foodstuff and household items. After a whole year of free grocery shopping for your home, you get a whopping 70% of your capital back!
