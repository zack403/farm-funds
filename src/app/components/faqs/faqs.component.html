What is Farmfunds Africa?

Farmfunds Africa (Farmify Agro Innovations Ltd: RC 1627509) is an agritech Company focused at empowering African Farmers whilst enabling individual investors earn profits on their investment capital which ultimately helps in strengthening global food security. We pride as Africa’s fastest growing Agritech Company.

How does it work?

We handle the entire agric value chain "from start to finish" while you sit back and enjoy returns after harvest.

How much is a farm unit?

A unit of Farm varies. It ranges from N40,000 – N1,000,000. You can decide the number of units you want to subscribe to.

Can I visit your farm?

Yes. Once a quarter, we hold a #VillageMeeting which is geared at connecting our farmfunders with farmers to build healthier relationships, and more importantly, enable our farmfunders see how their partnership is affecting real people and effecting economic growth. We also use that opportunity to take an helicopter farm tour for Farmfunders who subscribes to 20units and above.

What is the duration of farm?

All our available farms have their farm cycles which ranges between 100 days to 270 days (9 months). Your selected farm determines your partnership maturity.

Can I withdraw funds before maturity?

No You cannot. However, in extreme cases (death) , your next of kin will be paid your full partnership funds and returns at the end of farm cycle.

At farm maturity, how do I get my funds back?

At maturity, your partnership funds plus its accrued profit is paid back to your local bank account. At this point you can either choose to resubscribe or walk away happily.

How secured is my partnership fund?

All our farms and farmers have a comprehensive insurance cover to mitigate all risks. Your partnership with us is secured. We have a relationship with Leadway Assurance Plc and NAIC (Nigerian Agricultural Insurance Cooperation)
